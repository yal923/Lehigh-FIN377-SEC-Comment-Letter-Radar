You state that you expect to recognize approximately 55% of the $5.4 billion contracted but unsatisfied performance obligations, excluding non-cancelable commitments, as revenue over the next 12 months. Please revise future filings to disclose when the remaining 45% will be recognized on a quantitative basis using time bands that would be most appropriate for the duration of the remaining performance obligations or by providing qualitative information.
