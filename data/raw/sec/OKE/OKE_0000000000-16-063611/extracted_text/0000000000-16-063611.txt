Please tell us how you determined presentation of this subtotal is appropriate, including how you considered whether this is a non-GAAP measure and how you considered the applicability of SAB Topic 11:B. In future filings, please disclose a more detailed explanation of how the mix of contracts changed and how this change impacted your net margin. A more detailed explanation of this segment’s POP with fee contracts elsewhere in your filing may provide greater transparency when explaining how the contract mix affects this segment’s profitability.
