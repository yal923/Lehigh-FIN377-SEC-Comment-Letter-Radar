In response to your comments, we have reproduced your comments below in boldface, italic type and set forth our response immediately below each comment. Form 10-Q for the Quarterly Period Ended September 30, 2017 Management’s Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations Non-GAAP and Other Financial Disclosures, page 183 1. “Operating revenues”, “operating expenses” and “operating earnings” that you identify as non-GAAP measures appear to be titles that are the same as, or confusingly similar to, those used for GAAP financial measures and precluded by Item 10(e)(ii)(E) of Regulation S-K. As such, please confirm to us that you will revise the titles of these measures in future filings to provide an appropriate description of each measure that complies with Item 10(e). Management’s Response: We confirm that, to the extent we include such non-GAAP measures in our filings beginning with 2017 fourth quarter and full-year disclosures, we will revise the titles of those measures to provide an appropriate description of each measure that complies with Item 10(e).
