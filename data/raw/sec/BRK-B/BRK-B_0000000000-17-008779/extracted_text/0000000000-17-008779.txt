Refer to ASC 944-40-50-4H. Mr. Marc D. Hamburg Berkshire Hathaway Inc. March 15, 2017 Page 3 6. Please tell us why the net unpaid losses and allocated loss adjustment expenses for accident years before the oldest year presented in each of your claims development tables is unaudited. ASC 944-40-50-4B identifies all periods presented in the disclosure about claims development that precede the most recent reporting period as supplementary information. However, the aggregate remaining liability for unpaid losses and allocated loss adjustment expenses for accident years prior to the oldest year presented must be audited as it is necessary to reconcile the ending obligation in each claims development table to the liability on the ending balance sheet.
