Amendment No. 1 to Draft Registration Statement Risk Factors, page 23 1. We note your risk factor disclosure that if the Court of Chancery of the State of Delaware dismisses an action for lack of jurisdiction, the action may be brought in the federal court for the District of Delaware. This is inconsistent with your disclosure in the Description of Carrier Capital Stock that if no state court located within the State of Delaware has jurisdiction over a specified claim, the sole and exclusive forum for the claim will be the federal district court for the District of Delaware. Please reconcile this disclosure and clarify whether your exclusive forum provision requires a claim under the Securities Act or Exchange Act to be dismissed in state court prior to bringing the claim in federal court.
