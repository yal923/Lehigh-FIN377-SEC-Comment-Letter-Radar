Form 10-K for the Fiscal Year Ended December 31, 2017 Item 7A - Qualitative and Quantitative Disclosures about Market Risk, page 43 1. We note that you are exposed to the following market risks: interest rate risk related to your debt instruments, foreign currency exchange rate risk related to your construction contracts denominated in euros, and fuel price risk related to your operations. Please revise your future filings to disclose for all market risks the quantitative information for the preceding fiscal year along with the reasons for material changes in amounts from the preceding year.
