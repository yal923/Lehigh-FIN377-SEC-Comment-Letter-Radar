You disclose that revenue from performance obligations related to services is recognized over time, as the performance obligation is satisfied. Tell us what types of services these represent and the amount of revenue generated from services. To the extent such amounts are greater than 10% of total revenue tell us your consideration for presenting these separately on the income statement. Refer to Rule 5-03 of Regulation S-X.
