In future filings, please revise to separately disclose the amount of related party transactions on the face of the consolidated balance sheet and statement of income. Refer to Rule 4-08(k) of Regulation S-X. Response: In response to the Staff’s comment, in future filings the Company will separately disclose on the face of the financial statements the related party transaction amounts that are currently disclosed in Note 5 of the financial statements in the 10-K.
