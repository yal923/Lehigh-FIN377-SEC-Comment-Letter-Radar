Form 10-Q for the Quarterly Period Ended June 30, 2018 Note 2. Accounting Standards and Significant Accounting Policies Revenue Recognition Software Licenses and Royalties, page 6 1. Your disclosures appear to indicate that royalty revenue is not recognized until you receive notice of amounts earned which is in the quarter subsequent to the quarter the royalty is actually earned. Please tell us how this complies with ASC 606-10-55-65. However, we also note that the disclosure on page 7 of your Form 10-Q for the quarterly period ended March 31, 2018 indicates that royalties are recognized on an estimated basis in the period earned and are then trued up when you receive notice of amounts earned.
