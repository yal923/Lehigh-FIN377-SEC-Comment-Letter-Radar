Form 10-K for the Fiscal Year Ended December 31, 2018 Notes to the Consolidated and Combined Financial Statements Note 16 - Segment Information, page 89 1. We note that you made reference to, and defined, Total Segment EBITDA, a non-GAAP measure, within the context of ASC 280 disclosures. Please note, non-GAAP financial measures should not be presented in the financial statements. Please remove that characterization. Refer to guidance in Item 10(e)(1)(ii)(C) of Regulation S-K. In addition, the current caption “Total Segment EBITDA” is the same as, or confusingly similar to, Segment EBITDA, which is a GAAP financial measure for segment performance pursuant to ASC 280. It is also confusingly similar to EBITDA, but contains adjustments for items other than interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization. If you present a similar measure in future filings, please ensure that measures calculated differently from EBITDA are not characterized as EBITDA.
