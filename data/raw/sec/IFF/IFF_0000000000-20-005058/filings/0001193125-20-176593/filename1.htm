General

 

1.

Please amend this registration statement to address any applicable comments issued on the Nutrition & Biosciences, Inc. registration statement on Form S-4 and Form S-1 filed on May 7, 2020.

Response:

The Company acknowledges the Staff’s comment and advises the Staff that the Registration Statement has been revised to address the applicable comments issued on the Nutrition & Biosciences, Inc. (“N&B”) registration statement on Form S-4 and Form S-1 filed on May 7, 2020 (the “N&B Registration Statement”). Please see Amendment No. 1 filed herewith and N&B’s response letter as of the date hereof to the Commission’s letter dated June 5, 2020 with respect to the N&B Registration Statement, which includes responses to the Staff’s comments on the N&B Registration Statement.
