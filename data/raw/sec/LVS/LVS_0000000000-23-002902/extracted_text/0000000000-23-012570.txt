We note your response to comment 1. We continue to believe your adjustments for hold- normalized casino revenue and expense to arrive at consolidated hold-normalized adjusted property EBITDA, hold-normalized adjusted property EBITDA for Macao Operations and Marina Bay Sands, and hold-normalized adjusted net income (loss) from continuing operations attributable to LVS are inconsistent with Question 100.04 of the Non-GAAP Financial Measures Compliance and Disclosure Interpretations. Please revise to remove the non-GAAP measures that include adjustments for hold-normalized casino revenue and expense.
