Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2024 Critical Accounting Policies and Estimates Accounting for Long-Lived Assets - Impairment Evaluation, page 48 1. We note your disclosure that in light of the impairment of the Fiber segment goodwill of approximately $5 billion, the Company conducted a review during the fourth quarter of 2024, including analysis of both market-level and segment-level information, and determined there was no impairment of its Fiber segment long-lived assets. In performing your impairment testing, please tell us how you considered the guidance in ASC 350-20-35-31 in performing your recoverability test for your long- lived assets prior to your goodwill impairment test and how that may have impacted such analysis, if any.
